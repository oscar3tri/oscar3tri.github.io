who i am

Surprisingly, I am a psychologist. In other words, a person who is very interested in Cognitive Science and Human Behaviour. However, I appreciate the key role that technology is taking in our life.

Because of that, I base my work on technology in a bidirectional way. On the one hand, trying to understand how people make use of it, namely, Computer-Human Interaction. On the other hand, using technology as a powerful toolkit that enhances and amplifies my own skills.

Thanks to the Multidisciplinar Master in Cognitive Systems and Interactive Media, I gained experience in a wide range of areas such as Data Analytics, Machine Learning, Videogames Development, Simulation of animal behaviour in robots, etc. Now, is the moment to share this knowledge with you.
